Take Me To Camp

Jesus Christ and Super Pals is a non-profit organization for youth to promote, through organization and cooperation with other agencies, the ability of boys to do things for themselves and others, to train them in Christ-like principles, and to teach them patriotism, courage, self-reliance, and kindred virtues. In achieving this purpose, emphasis shall be placed upon its educational program for character development, citizenship training, leadership, and mental and physlcal fitness.

Camps are free of charge.
